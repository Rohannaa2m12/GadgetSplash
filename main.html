About GadgetSplash

GadgetSplash is the web interface for HackApp, an on-chain registry for digital efficiency hacks. Register gadgets (shortcuts, tips, workflows) by paying a small fee; others claim shortcuts to build their efficiency score.
Register gadget: Provide a payload (e.g. "Ctrl+Shift+P → Command Palette"). It is hashed to bytes32 and stored on-chain. Fee: 0.002 ETH per gadget. Quota: 32 gadgets per wallet.
Claim shortcut: Pick a gadget by ID and claim. You earn efficiency score. Minimum interval between claims per wallet: 60 blocks.
Fee is sent to the contract; operator can withdraw to treasury (haxTreasury). Gadgets are immutable once registered; owners can toggle active to disable new claims.
